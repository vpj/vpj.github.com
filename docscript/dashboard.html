Dashboard

The dashboard gives a summary of all the tests. This appears as you log into the system. The dashboard gives a set of tiles and each tile gives you information like the results of a single test, information about number of records or the filters that are active.

Filters Tile

As you log in, the Filters tile would be blank and would have no filters set as shown above.

Payments Tile

It is advised to view transactions when the number of transactions drop to less than 100. If not you may not be taking full advantages of the functionality of Forestpin.

As you login, the Payments Tile would give you the total number of payments and the total value of the payments. There are 265,176 Invoice transactions that are in the dataset, the total value of these transactions is 5,762,313,942.67.

Top Vendors Tile

This tile shows the results of a test, here the top three vendors based on the number of invoices are shown. You can click on this test and see the complete test. Normally if the results are shown in red it is recommended to investigate further.

In the above example Pomona has almost four times the invoices as compared to Paynesville. By looking at the dashboard you can prioritize the areas you need to investigate further. Most of the tiles are clickable and takes you to the actual test.

Detailed View - Individual Vendor

The vendors are sorted by the Invoice count and shown here. You can also see Payment Total, Payment Low, Payment High, Payment Avg in the screen. Results of many tests can be viewed by using the scroll bar.
